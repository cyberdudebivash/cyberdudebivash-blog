Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SENTINEL APEX intelligence subscriptions.

What exactly is "pre-disclosure intelligence"? +
Pre-disclosure intelligence means Pro and Enterprise subscribers receive detailed threat analysis — including technical breakdown, proof-of-concept indicators, affected versions, and recommended mitigations — up to 48 hours before CVEs are publicly announced or added to the NVD. This gives your SOC team critical lead time to patch, segment, or deploy compensating controls before threat actors weaponize vulnerabilities.
What IOC formats are available on the Pro plan? +
Pro subscribers receive IOC feeds in both plain JSON and STIX 2.1 format. Each daily IOC bundle includes IP addresses, domain indicators, file hashes (MD5, SHA-1, SHA-256), URL patterns, and YARA rules where applicable. SIEM-ready Sigma rules are also included for deployment directly into Splunk, Elastic, Microsoft Sentinel, and Chronicle.
How does the threat feed API work? +
Pro subscribers receive a dedicated REST API endpoint with bearer token authentication. You can query the latest CVE data, IOC feeds, SIEM rules, and threat reports programmatically. Pro accounts include 10,000 API calls per month. Enterprise accounts receive unlimited API access with priority rate limits and direct STIX/TAXII feed integration for native SIEM/SOAR ingestion.
Can I cancel the Pro plan at any time? +
Yes. Monthly Pro subscriptions can be cancelled at any time — you retain access until the end of your current billing period with no cancellation fees. Annual subscriptions are non-refundable after 14 days but can be cancelled to prevent renewal. Contact bivash@cyberdudebivash.com for any billing adjustments.
What does a "dedicated security analyst" mean in the Enterprise plan? +
Enterprise clients are paired with a named SENTINEL APEX analyst who understands your organization's infrastructure, technology stack, and threat profile. Your analyst proactively surfaces relevant threats, delivers tailored intelligence briefings, and is available for scheduled calls to discuss emerging risks. Response SLAs are negotiated based on your organization's requirements.
Do you offer a free trial for the Pro plan? +
Yes. Email bivash@cyberdudebivash.com with subject "SOC Pro Trial" and we will set up a 7-day full-access Pro trial at no cost, including API access, IOC feeds, and pre-disclosure report delivery. No credit card required to start the trial.
Is the intelligence vendor-agnostic? +
Completely. SENTINEL APEX threat intelligence is platform-agnostic and can integrate with any SIEM, SOAR, or TIP platform including Microsoft Sentinel, Splunk Enterprise Security, Elastic Security, IBM QRadar, Palo Alto XSOAR, and others. STIX/TAXII integration is available on Enterprise for native feed ingestion without manual export steps.
